I decided to have this experience when I realised that Spanish could be a killer for my exam results. Learnings:

  • I improved my Spanish.
  • I left the world of the prep school (to keep a distance, not to be obsessed, simply the choice not to limit my life to the prep school).
  • I met new people, I discovered new lifestyles which show me the extent of the possibilities of existence and which question and relativised my own choices: father-son relationship, meal schedule, involvement of the whole family in the meal, the garden and the community, Gaspard a young man of my age who manages a company (truck, finance, leadership, projects)
